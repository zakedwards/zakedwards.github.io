              Employment

              2022—2025

              Diemaker at Firmin & Sons, Birmingham, England

              General duties: production of vector artwork as per client specification, conversion to plot files according to machine specification, and CNC machine operation to engrave master patterns; printing of 3D master patterns from stereolithographic models; hand crafting of steel dies using traditional and modern techniques, including lathe turning, pantograph cutting, spark erosion, engraving and benching (hand-tooling/polishing); fashioning of specialised toolbits; and production of technical engineering drawings (example).
              Created product visualisations/illustrations for prospective customers, in addition to novel designs pertaining to multiple sectors of manufacturing, including for Kashket & Partners of London.

              2018—2021

              Freelance Artist (Portraitist), Commission

              Created portrait works (oil-on-canvas) according to commissionees’ desiderata and specifications; additional occasional freelance graphic design work.
