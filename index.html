Major Academic Works

5/5/2016
'An Optimised Genetic Algorithm for Competitive Robocode Agents'
We explore how orthodox genetic techniques can be conducted upon binary expression trees to induce 'robots' that perform competitively in the simulated Robocode environment.
Module: CS255 Artificial Intelligence
9/3/2016
'Identifying Emergent Behavioural Patterns in the Enron Corpus via Exploratory Data Analysis'
We present a system to facilitate the investigation, and subsequent visualisation, of patterns and structures present within large communications datasets.
Module: CS261 Software Engineering
24/11/2015
'Implementing the Two Phase Commit Protocol in Java'
We employ the two-phase commit transaction protocol to query the stock availability of databases, and perform operations contingent on this availability, via the implementation of Threads for concurrent execution.
Module: CS241 Operating Systems and Computer Networks
7/5/2015
'Programmatically Generating 3D Models in C'
By employing geometric relations of proportionality and the iterative generation of elementary structures in C, we show complex structures to be generable and printable upon inputted dimensional parameters.
Module: CS132 Computer Organisation and Architecture
